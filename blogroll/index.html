Here are some cool websites I think are worth sharing!

pwn.college, a really good open source site for binary exploitation and cybersecurity fundamentals
Cryptohack, a free site for learning CTF cryptography
Idktheflag, my CTF team's website! (lots of good documentation narrated by yours truly)
Wargame Nexus, a directory of good and (mostly) free CTF resources in case the above weren't enough
riverxia.vercel.app, my friend and co-captain River's website
MIT Opencourseware, the not-so-secret secret to how I get good grades
freemediaheckyeah, a directory for free internet resources. (NOTE: I DO NOT CONDONE PIRACY)
Neocities, a static web host with a lot of very cool sites that follow arguably better classical conventions
You are not a Gadget, a thought provoking book I'd recommend to anyone who uses the internet
Moonlight Shadow, a song I really enjoy (hopefully this doesn't expose my "real age")
Broadway on Tour, check out if there's a play in your city! (I love theatre)
Legal Information Institute, a directory of legal codes across the world. It's important we abide by the law, especially in cybersecurity
